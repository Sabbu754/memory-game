Memory Game

Match a pair of 2 identical cards in this game!

All cards are shuffled and then presented face-down, you can flip 2 cards per turn to find the matching pairs. The cards will be shown to you for 1-2 seconds.

A timer and a star ranking will display your success in the game. Good luck!
